How to Fill the Form

            - After getting on the webpage, click on the form button. Select the year for which the form needs to be filled.
            - Fill in the form, add all the required documents, select undertakings, and submit the form.
            - You can preview your form after submission.

How to View the Lists

            : Go to Perspective Year And Access and download both provisional and final lists Same For Important Dates .

Student:

  1. - You can fill only one form of a one year in a single browser. If you fill a second, the first form will be lost.
  2. - You need to view your form in the same browser where you fill the form.
  3. - Nothing different between Mining Form. It is the same as the Normal Department.
