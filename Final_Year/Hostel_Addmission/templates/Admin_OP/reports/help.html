Instructions Page

            In this project of the online hostel management website, we aim to streamline the admission process and reduce the paperwork involved. Our goal is to alleviate the stress on both the managing committee and students by providing a user-friendly platform for hostel admission.
    
            The website functions as follows:
            
            - Home Page: Displays information about the managing committee, new notifications, available options, and glimpses of the hostel.
            
            - Student Admission: Forms are available year-wise in the student form section. Students can fill the form, upload necessary documents, and submit it after thorough rechecking.
            
            - List Notifications: After submitting the form, students can check the scrolling notification bar on the home page for updates on the admission list.
            
            - Admin Panel: Admin login ensures confidentiality and allows the admin to control form and list displays, as well as notify about important dates.
            
            - List Generation: Admin allocates seats based on categories, generating lists accordingly. The admin can search and view details of applied students.
            
            - Student Access: Once notified, students can view and download the generated lists from the notification bar.
            
            - Admission Process: Successfully selected students visit the hostel to complete the admission process and commence their academic journey.
